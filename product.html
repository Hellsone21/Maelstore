Nike SF Air Force 1 "Rush Coral"
34 34 34 34
The Nike SF Air Force 1 ‘Rush Coral’ showcases a two-tone design on a hybrid silhouette that blends the Air Force 1 with the military-inspired Special Field Boot. The combination suede and ballistic mesh upper is finished in khaki, topped with an extremely high collar fastened with two ankle straps—one in Rush Coral, the other in tonal khaki. An additional hit of Coral appears on the rubber outsole.
